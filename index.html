About

Who is ZAF?

I'm a solo developer from the Netherlands building mobile games on the side while learning and experimenting with new technologies. I work under the name ZAF Development Studio — no big team, no investors, just passion and a lot of trial and error.

My goal is simple: make small, fun games that people enjoy during a bus ride or a few minutes of downtime. Nothing overly complex — just solid mechanics, clean design, and honest fun.
